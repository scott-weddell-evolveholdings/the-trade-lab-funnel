# The Trades Lab — Offer & Positioning (source of truth)

**Audience:** UK trade businesses broadly — plumbers, electricians, builders, roofers, bathroom
fitters, heating, landscaping. Per-niche funnels narrow to one trade; the main site stays broad.

## The offer (approved framing — use this, not older copy)
NOT a free build. The free thing is a **bespoke demo we build first**:
> "See your new site before you pay a penny. We build a real, working demo of your business —
> your trade, your town — for free. Love it? It's yours, from £1,500. Don't? Keep it for ideas."

- **Free** = a bespoke demo (we build it first).
- **Paid** = done-for-you site **from £1,500**, one-off, client owns it outright.
- **Scarcity** = one site per trade, per town. **No fake "X towns claimed" counters.**
- Drop all "free in 2026 / free case study / £997 value" language.

## Price-advantage framing (important)
Frame the saving as **systemisation**, NOT "no monthly fees":
> "Agencies charge £5,000+ for this level of animation because they build from scratch; we've
> systemised it and pass the saving on — from £1,500."
Do **NOT** position against monthly fees — the back-end upsell is ongoing Google Ads + SEO
(monthly), so anti-monthly-fee copy undercuts our own revenue.

## Proof (no trade testimonials yet — do NOT fabricate them)
Use **founder/team pedigree**: "We don't just build websites. We engineer revenue."
Stats: £6.8M+ AUD ad spend managed (convert & round for UK), 60,000+ customers generated,
1,000+ campaigns, 10+ yrs. Plus real dashboard "receipts". Framed as marketing capability —
NOT trade-client results. The before/after gallery is a **feature demo** ("this is how we'll
show off your work"), captions marked "Example".
