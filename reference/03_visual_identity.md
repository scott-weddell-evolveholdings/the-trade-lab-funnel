# PoolMasters Visual Identity

## Overall Visual Feel
PoolMasters should feel like:
- Premium local service brand
- Clean water
- High trust
- Professional operator
- Modern business growth system
- Blue-collar success upgraded
- Practical but polished

The visual identity should communicate:
- Mastery
- Cleanliness
- Trust
- Authority
- Momentum
- Water
- Local business growth

## Design Direction
PoolMasters should look like:

**A premium pool service company + a modern marketing platform + a business growth system.**

It should not look like:
- A generic marketing agency
- A cheap contractor website
- A cartoon pool brand
- A boring corporate SaaS tool
- A random local service template

## Color Palette

### Deep Navy
Hex: `#061E33`

Use for:
- Header backgrounds
- Footer
- Premium sections
- Strong buttons
- Main text on light backgrounds

Meaning:
- Trust
- Authority
- Stability
- Professionalism

### Pool Blue
Hex: `#009FE3`

Use for:
- Primary accents
- Buttons
- Links
- Icons
- Highlights

Meaning:
- Water
- Freshness
- Clarity
- Energy

### Aqua Glow
Hex: `#35D6FF`

Use for:
- Gradients
- Hover states
- Glow effects
- Secondary highlights

Meaning:
- Clean water
- Motion
- Modernity

### White
Hex: `#FFFFFF`

Use for:
- Clean backgrounds
- Text on dark backgrounds
- Card surfaces

### Mist Gray
Hex: `#F4F8FB`

Use for:
- Light section backgrounds
- Feature areas
- Card zones

### Slate Text
Hex: `#334155`

Use for:
- Body copy
- Subtext
- Supporting text

### Deep Charcoal
Hex: `#0F172A`

Use for:
- Headlines on white backgrounds
- High-contrast body copy

### Master Gold
Hex: `#F5B942`

Use sparingly for:
- Certified badges
- Premium highlights
- Status symbols
- Small icons

Gold should feel like status, not luxury decoration.

## Gradient System

### Primary Gradient
Use for buttons and visual highlights:

```css
background: linear-gradient(135deg, #009FE3 0%, #35D6FF 100%);
```

### Premium Dark Gradient
Use for hero and CTA sections:

```css
background: linear-gradient(135deg, #061E33 0%, #0A3558 55%, #009FE3 140%);
```

### Water Glow
Use as subtle background accent:

```css
background: radial-gradient(circle at top right, rgba(53, 214, 255, 0.28), transparent 40%);
```

## Typography

### Recommended Headline Font
Use:
- Sora
- Montserrat
- Manrope
- Oswald

Best recommendation:
**Sora**

It feels modern, strong, premium, and clean.

### Recommended Body Font
Use:
- Inter
- Open Sans
- Roboto

Best recommendation:
**Inter**

It is clean, highly readable, and modern.

## Logo Direction
The logo should communicate:
- Mastery
- Water
- Professional service
- Status
- Business growth

Best logo/icon elements:
- Letter M
- Crown
- Wave
- Shield
- Circle badge
- Water ripple

Recommended icon:
**M + crown + wave**

This reinforces:
- “Masters”
- Pool/water
- Premium identity
- Easy recognition

## Logo Rules
Do:
- Use clean backgrounds
- Keep spacing around the logo
- Use navy, blue, white, or monochrome versions
- Use icon-only for small placements

Do not:
- Stretch the logo
- Use too many effects
- Place logo over busy images
- Make it cartoonish
- Add random colors

## Iconography
Use:
- Clean line icons
- Water-inspired shapes
- Checkmarks
- Calendar icons
- Lead notification icons
- Phone/message icons
- Badge icons
- Shield/crown symbols

Avoid:
- Cartoon pool floats
- Cheap clipart
- Overly complex icons
- Random tech icons
