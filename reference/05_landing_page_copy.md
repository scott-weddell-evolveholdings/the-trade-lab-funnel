## Problem Section

### Headline
Great Pool Cleaners Shouldn’t Have to Chase Customers

### Copy
You already know how to keep pools clean. The harder part is keeping your schedule full.

If your business depends only on referrals, random calls, or outdated marketing, growth becomes unpredictable.

PoolMasters helps fix that with a simple marketing system built around how pool service customers actually search, compare, and book.

### Problem Cards

#### Inconsistent Leads
Some weeks are busy. Other weeks are quiet. That makes it hard to plan and grow.

#### Weak Online Presence
If your website or Google profile looks outdated, customers may choose someone else.

#### Too Many Price Shoppers
When your business does not look different, customers compare you only on price.

#### Missed Follow-Up
If leads are not followed up with quickly, they move on to the next company.

#### Referral Dependence
Referrals are great, but they should not be your only growth strategy.

#### Competitors Look More Professional
The best pool cleaner does not always win. The business that looks more trusted often does.
