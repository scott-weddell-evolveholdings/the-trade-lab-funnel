# PoolMasters Landing Page Brief

## Page Goal
The landing page should convince pool service business owners that PoolMasters understands their business and can help them get more leads, book more jobs, and look more professional.

## Primary Conversion Goal
Get the visitor to request a Pool Growth Plan or book a strategy call.

## Recommended Primary CTA
**Get My Pool Growth Plan**

## Secondary CTA
**See How It Works**

## Main Page Promise
PoolMasters helps pool service companies get more local leads, book more jobs, and build a professional brand that stands out in their market.

## Page Feeling
The page should feel:
- Premium
- Clean
- Trustworthy
- Direct
- Local-business focused
- Professional
- Simple to understand
- Not overly technical

## Design Style
Use:
- Navy and aqua palette
- Clean section spacing
- Strong headlines
- White and mist-gray cards
- Premium gradients
- Subtle water-inspired glows
- Rounded cards
- Floating result cards
- Realistic pool service imagery

Avoid:
- Clutter
- Generic marketing agency visuals
- Overloaded funnel design
- Cartoon pool graphics
- Excessive tech dashboards
- Too much jargon
