# PoolMasters AI Landing Page Builder Instructions

## Role
You are building a premium landing page for PoolMasters, a marketing and growth system for pool service businesses.

## Main Objective
Create a high-converting landing page that helps pool service business owners understand that PoolMasters can help them get more local leads, book more jobs, and look more professional.

## Brand Feeling
The page should feel:
- Premium
- Clean
- Professional
- Trustworthy
- Local-business friendly
- Results-driven
- Confident
- Simple

## Audience
The audience is pool service business owners, including solo pool cleaners and small pool service companies.

They want:
- More leads
- More booked jobs
- More recurring service clients
- Better online presence
- Less reliance on referrals
- A more professional business

## Main Message
PoolMasters helps pool service businesses get more local leads, book more jobs, and build a professional brand that stands out.

## Main Headline
Build a Booked-Out Pool Business

## Main CTA
Get My Pool Growth Plan

## Page Structure
Build the page with these sections:

1. Hero
2. Problem
3. Identity Shift
4. Solution / Growth System
5. Offer
6. Visual Proof / Mockups
7. Before and After
8. Who It Is For
9. Process
10. Final CTA

## Hero Instructions
Use a premium dark navy gradient background with aqua highlights.

Hero content:
- Badge: Marketing Systems for Pool Service Businesses
- Headline: Build a Booked-Out Pool Business
- Subheadline: PoolMasters helps pool service companies get more local leads, book more jobs, and build a professional brand that stands out in their market.
- Primary CTA: Get My Pool Growth Plan
- Secondary CTA: See How It Works
- Trust bullets:
  - Built specifically for pool service businesses
  - Local lead capture and follow-up
  - Professional brand upgrade

Hero visual:
Create a premium image/mockup area showing a pool service owner, clean pool, and floating result cards such as:
- New Pool Cleaning Lead
- Appointment Booked
- Follow-Up Sent

## Visual Style Instructions
Use:
- Deep navy: #061E33
- Pool blue: #009FE3
- Aqua glow: #35D6FF
- Mist gray: #F4F8FB
- White: #FFFFFF
- Slate text: #334155
- Gold accent: #F5B942 sparingly

Use:
- Rounded cards
- Soft shadows
- Clean spacing
- Strong typography
- Premium gradients
- Subtle water-inspired effects

Avoid:
- Cartoon graphics
- Cheap local business templates
- Generic marketing agency stock images
- Overly complex tech visuals
- Too much jargon

## Typography
Use:
- Sora or Montserrat for headlines
- Inter for body copy

## Copy Rules
Keep copy:
- Simple
- Clear
- Direct
- Practical
- Outcome-focused

Avoid:
- Marketing jargon
- SaaS jargon
- Overhyped guru copy
- Long paragraphs

## Conversion Priorities
The page must sell:
1. The outcome: more leads and booked jobs
2. The identity: becoming a Pool Master
3. The system: lead capture, brand upgrade, follow-up, campaigns

## Final CTA
End with:

Headline:
Ready to Become the Pool Company Everyone Calls First?

Copy:
If you are ready to get more local leads, book more jobs, and build a more professional pool service business, PoolMasters can help you put the system in place.

Button:
Get My Pool Growth Plan
