# PoolMasters Social Ads and Creative Guide

## Core Ad Message
PoolMasters helps pool service businesses get more local leads, book more jobs, and stop relying only on referrals.

## Ad Creative Style
Ads should feel:
- Clean
- Bold
- Local-business focused
- Results-driven
- Easy to understand
- Premium but practical

Use:
- Sparkling pools
- Pool service owners
- Lead notification graphics
- Booked calendar visuals
- Before/after transformations
- Bold simple text overlays

Avoid:
- Too much text
- Generic marketing imagery
- Cheesy contractor photos
- Cartoonish pool graphics
- Corporate stock photos

## Creative Rules
- Use short text overlays.
- Make the main promise readable in 2 seconds.
- Use high-contrast navy and aqua.
- Keep visuals clean.
- Show real-world pool business context.
- Avoid fake hype.
